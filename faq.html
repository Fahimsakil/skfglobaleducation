Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers. If you don’t see what you’re looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

How do I apply through SKF Global Education?

You can start your application by filling out the form on our Apply Now page. Our counselors will contact you to guide you through the rest of the process.

Which countries can I apply to?

We currently support applications to universities in the UK, Canada, Australia, and the USA.

Is your service free for students?

Yes, our counseling and university application services are free for students. We are supported by our university partners.

Do you help with visa applications?

Yes. We provide step-by-step guidance for your visa application and required documentation after receiving your offer letter.

Can I apply for scholarships?

Absolutely! We help eligible students identify and apply for scholarships available through universities and other organizations.
